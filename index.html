About KGI

With a yearning desire to provide world class educational opportunities to meet global challenges, The Naba Digant Educational Trust (NDET) established a variety of learning environments under the aegis of the Koustuv Group of Institutions (KGI). The academy has been vital in establishing KGI. At KGI, we share the philosophy of creating merit, through a collaborative atmosphere tactically designed and structured to bring out and amplify the talents of all its students. Students are nurtured and encouraged to integrate academic inputs focused on real-life scenarios. Our State-of-the-art campus with its latest facilities creates a pool of knowledge-driven education. In addition to this, our co-curricular and extra-curricular activities help in exploring the creative facets of our students.

The first step towards the goal was College of Engineering Bhubaneswar (COEB), established in the year 1999, under the leadership of Er. Prabhat Ranjan Mallick, Chairman KGI. This was further expended by establishing the Koustuv Institute of Self Domain (KISD) in 2001, Koustuv Business School (KBS) in 2006, Koustuv School of Engineering (KSE) in 2006, Koustuv Institute of Science (KIS) in 2007, Naxatra Institute of Media Studies (NIMS) in 2009, Koustuv Institute of Professional Studies (KIPS) in 2018, Koustuv Research Institute of Medical Studies (KRIMS) in 2020.

The infrastructure of KGI offers the finest in resources and facilities, which meets the terms with university & AICTE requirements. A dual approach to education allows for holistic development of its students. This is coupled with a focus on team work and team spirit in order to make the students successful in the competitive professional world. High emphasis is also laid on assisting students to recognize and develop their strengths for success in their professions as well as in life. Our vision of imparting admirable education in the present era of educational doldrums enhances our eminence.

GOAL

To impart the best possible, industry-validated technical and professional education.

To elevate and strengthen the status of the Institute through continuous assessment and improvement of programs, curriculum and delivery.

To emerge as a pioneer institute in the field of imparting quality technical and professional education.

To obtain & maintain National Accreditation for the institute and its programs.

To provide excellent human resources equipped with best technical and moral values.
